# Funding Opportunities for FairBite

Research conducted: 2026-05-15

## EU Programs

### European Social Fund Plus (ESF+) - **HIGH PRIORITY**

- Main EU instrument for social innovation and cooperative economy
- Distributed at national/regional level in Greece
- Social Innovation+ initiative for transnational cooperation
- Investment potential: Up to €500,000
- **Action:** Contact regional ESF+ managing authority in Greece (Ministry of
  Labor)
- Links:
  - [ESF+ Main Site](https://european-social-fund-plus.ec.europa.eu/en)
  - [EU Funding & Tenders Portal - ESF](https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities/portal/screen/programmes/esf)
  - [Social Innovation Fund Portal](https://eufundingportal.eu/social-innovation-fund/)

### Horizon Europe 2026-2027

- Total budget: €14 billion across all programs
- Requirement: Usually requires consortia of 3+ organizations
- **Best work programmes for FairBite:**
  - **WP5 - Culture, Creativity & Inclusive Society:** Social innovation,
    participatory governance, community-based approaches
  - **WP9 - Food Systems:** Place-based, circular models in food systems where
    cooperatives play a key role
  - **WP10 - European Innovation Ecosystems:** Recognizes collective ownership
    and mission-driven capital
- **Action:** Need to identify consortium partners (universities, other co-ops,
  NGOs)
- Links:
  - [Horizon Europe Overview](https://research-and-innovation.ec.europa.eu/funding/funding-opportunities/funding-programmes-and-open-calls/horizon-europe_en)
  - [Social Economy Guide 2026-2027](https://euclidnetwork.eu/2025/12/horizon-europe-2026-2027-whats-in-it-for-the-social-economy/)
  - [Social Economy Gateway](https://social-economy-gateway.ec.europa.eu/eu-funding-programmes/horizon-europe_en)

### Programme for Employment and Social Innovation (EaSI)

- Investments up to €500,000
- Supports scaling of social innovation
- Direct EU-level grants available
- Links:
  - [EaSI Information](https://eufundingportal.eu/social-innovation-fund/)

### URBACT Action Networks

- Funding: Up to €1 million
- Duration: 30 months
- Deadline: March 23, 2026 (CHECK IF STILL OPEN)
- Requirement: Network of cities (need Patras municipality partnership)
- Focus: Social inclusion, digitalization, climate action
- **Action:** Approach Patras municipality about partnership
- Links:
  - [URBACT Information](https://eufundingportal.eu/social-innovation-fund/)

## Greek National Programs

### Greek Startup Ecosystem Support

- €200 million fund for innovative startups (life sciences, health,
  sustainability)
- Tax incentives: Up to 50% for R&D
- €3.2 billion for digital infrastructure through 2027
- Max investment cap: €900,000 (tripled from €300,000)
- **Action:** Register with Elevate Greece platform
- Links:
  - [Elevate Greece](https://elevategreece.gov.gr/) - Official Greek startup
    ecosystem platform
  - [Greece Startup Funding Overview](https://www.goldenvisa-greece.com/greeces-startup-ecosystem-in-2026-maturity-momentum-and-a-new-era-of-incentives/)
  - [Enterprise Greece](https://www.enterprisegreece.gov.gr/)

### EU Co-Financed Greek Programs

- Check Enterprise Greece for latest programs
- Look for programs under Greece's National Recovery and Resilience Plan
- **Action:** Monitor Enterprise Greece website for new calls

## Best Single Target for Solo Founder

**ESF+ Social Innovation** grants at national level - designed for this type of
project, don't require large consortia.

## Notes

- Most Horizon Europe programs require 3+ organization consortia
- ESF+ distributed through national/regional authorities in Greece
- Greek startup programs may not recognize "cooperatives" as "startups" - check
  eligibility
- Cooperative legal structure may provide advantages for some programs
- Consider applying to multiple programs simultaneously (non-exclusive)
